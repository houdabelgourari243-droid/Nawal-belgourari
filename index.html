— Who I Am

A multidisciplinary
creative mind

I am Nawal Belgourari — a developer, educator, writer and community builder from El Bayadh, Sidi Sheikh, Algeria. My work lives at the intersection of code, language, and people.

My academic journey spans two universities and a grande ecole: degrees in Science & Technology Engineering, Computer Engineering, and I teach English Language as a professor — a rare combination that makes me equally at home in a lecture hall, a terminal, or a blank page.

As a writer, I tell stories that matter. As an activist, I invest in my community through associative action. I believe the most interesting things happen when disciplines collide.
